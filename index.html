Luxury property ownership should be a reward, not a burden. Yet, many owners find themselves trapped, spending their valuable time "managing their manager" instead of enjoying their investment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties do you manage?

We specialize in high-end residential estates, luxury second homes, and boutique multi-unit portfolios. We do not manage large-scale commercial or standard rental properties.

How is Lumina different from a standard property manager?

We are a white-glove service firm, not a management commodity. Our focus is on asset preservation and elevated experiences, not just rent collection. We provide proactive, comprehensive care with 100% transparency.

What is your pricing structure?

Our fees are tailored to the unique needs of your property and the level of service required. We provide a custom, all-inclusive proposal after your free consultation. Our pricing is transparent, with no hidden fees or markups.

Where do you operate?

We currently serve exclusive properties in select luxury markets. Contact us to discuss your property's location and how we can serve you.

What is your "transparent" platform?

It's a secure, owners-only portal where you can see real-time maintenance schedules, financial statements, guest bookings, and direct communication logs—all from your phone or desktop.
